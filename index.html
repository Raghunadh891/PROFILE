Sorting Items in a List

I can sort multiple items in 2 ways in a list:

A mobile phone consists of:

  • camera
  • display
  • battery
  • motherboard
  • sensors
  • processor

And many more things are combined together to form a mobile phone.

The above was an unorganized list.

A mobile phone consists of:

  1. camera
  2. display
  3. battery
  4. motherboard
  5. sensors
  6. processor

And many more things are combined together to form a mobile phone.

The above was an organized list.

There is not much difference in both, but the automatic numbering in the organized list.
